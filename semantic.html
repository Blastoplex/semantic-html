What is a Graflex?

Graflex was a manufacturer that gave its brand name to several models of camera.
The original lightsaber hilt carried by Mark Hamill in A New Hope was made from the flash attachment of a Graflex 3 cell flash unit, with the ridged grips made by gluing portions of hard plastic T-track used for cabinet sliding doors onto the cylinder

Mark Hamill

Mark Hamill is the actor who played Luke Skywalker in ANH, ESB, RotJ, TFA, TLJ and TRoS

Who owned the Graflex?

Anakin Skywalker

As a Jedi Anakin Skywalker first built the Graflex in Attack of the clones.

Obi-Wan Kenobi

After defeating Anakin on Mustafar Obi-wan Kenobi took the Graflex to gift to Luke.

Luke Skywalker

In the early sequences of A New Hope, Obi-wan trained Luke Skywalker in the art of using the lightsaber. The lightsaber was then lost in a duel with Darth Vader on Bespin when Luke had his hand cut off.

Fin

In The Force Awakens, Fin uses the Graflex to fight storm troopers and Kylo Ren.

Rey

After using the Graflex to defeat the Emperor in The Rise of Skywalker, Rey burries the lightsaber on Tatooine.
